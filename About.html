About The App

  • The app will alert the people stuck in dangerous situation who are unaware of the danger hereby saving their life and will guide them in further action to be taken (ex best route to escape the building, etc).



  • This app will also will also act as a medium to alert the respective authorities (Ex Fire Department in case of fire, Police Department in case of active shooter in school/college) so they can reach the destination on time and take immediate action.



  • This app will display the actions taken in case of an emergency in case any individual does not have prior knowledge of it so that he/she can know the procedure beforehand. (ex. CPR procedure in case of heart attack, etc).





Uses Of The App


  1. The app can be used in school/offices to save numerous lives.

  2. Increase in knowledge about android development and web development.

  3. The app will alerts the user, unknown of the danger.

  4. Our app can acts as a medium of contact between those who are stuck in the emergency.

  5. It is also easier to access, as compare to the traditional fire alarms system at the time of emergency.

  6. Through this app the user can contact the authorities during the emergency.
